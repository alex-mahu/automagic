BUG-014

Summary: [Accessibility] User can not close the "Meeting date" or "Created on" popovers using ESC

Severity: Medium
Priority: Minor

Description:
When the user presses ESC to close the "Meeting date" or "Created on" popovers nothing happens

Steps to reproduce:
1. Login to the application
2. Navigate to "Topic Hub"
3. Click on the "Meeting date" or "Created on" filters
4. Press ESC

Expected behavior:
Popovers close and focus is returned to the filter field that was open

Actual behavior:
The popovers do not close
