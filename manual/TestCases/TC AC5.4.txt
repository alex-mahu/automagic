TC AC5.4

Description:
On date pickers start date is not updated when user hovers over dates for "Created on" and there is a start date selected

Steps:
1. Login to the application
2. Navigate to "Topic Hub"
3. Click on the "Created on" date picker button
4. Click on a date (ex: 12 Jan 2023)
5. Hove mouse over other dates

Expected: start date no longer updates, remains fixed on the selected date (ex: 12 Jan 2023)
