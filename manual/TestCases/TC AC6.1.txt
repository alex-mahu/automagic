TC AC6.1

Description:
On date pickers end date is updated when user hovers over dates for "Meeting Date"

Steps:
1. Login to the application
2. Navigate to "Topic Hub"
3. Click on the "Meeting date" date picker button
4. Select a date (in the past) (ex 12 Jan 2023)
5. Hove mouse over other dates (ex 13 Jan 2023)

Expected: in the date picker component below the filters, the end date updates with the date the mouse is over (ex: 13 Jan 2023)
